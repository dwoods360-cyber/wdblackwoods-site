# What Coffee Demands Artifact Generation Brief

Production package for literary-archive artifacts tied to:

**What Coffee Demands: Volume One -- Hold the Earth**

This brief supports the later generation and approval of public downloadable artwork. It does not include manuscript text, private Reader Room material, or placeholder images.

## Master Style

A story-world artifact from What Coffee Demands, late 19th century, historically grounded, cinematic, tactile, slightly weathered, emotionally evocative, tied to a British family's journey from London and Southampton toward Massawa and inland Abyssinia. Warm muted palette, sepia-brown undertones, natural materials, subtle signs of handling and travel, realistic textures, no modern objects, no contemporary design language, no watermarks, no visible modern branding, no plastic, no fantasy styling. Suitable for a literary archive, public website, handout, or downloadable companion artifact collection.

## Production Notes

- Generate final artwork only after individual prompts are reviewed.
- Keep every artifact suitable for public archive use.
- Do not include private manuscript excerpts, Reader Room names, invitation data, or unpublished private-reader notes.
- Do not use modern trademarks, visible contemporary branding, plastic materials, fantasy props, or watermarks.
- Save only approved public artwork into `public/downloads/artifacts/`.
- Keep exploratory source material, references, layered files, and rejected generations out of git under `private/artifact-source/`.
