The opening chapters of What Coffee Demands

## Body

Hello @FirstName,

We spoke some time ago about the historical-fiction trilogy I have been developing, What Coffee Demands. You kindly gave me your email address because you were interested in reading a chapter or two when the opening was ready.

I have now prepared selected pages for a small group of early readers.

Your private reading link is below:

@InviteLink

Reading version: @ChapterVersion

Please read naturally. I would value your honest reaction—especially where the story pulled you in, where anything felt unclear, and whether you wanted to continue reading.

This link is intended for your private review.

Thank you again for your interest.

— W.D. Blackwoods

## Gmail Mail Merge Steps

1. Open Gmail on desktop.
2. Compose a new message.
3. Turn on Gmail mail merge beside the `To:` line, if available.
4. Choose `Add from a spreadsheet`.
5. Import the generated CSV through a spreadsheet.
6. Use the merge tags `@FirstName`, `@InviteLink`, and optionally `@ChapterVersion`.
7. Review the message carefully.
8. Send only to the intended early-reader group.

If Gmail mail merge is not available for the account, send individually reviewed messages using the generated CSV as the source. Do not add attachments, do not use BCC, and keep `@InviteLink` as a plain-text URL on its own line.
